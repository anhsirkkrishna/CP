I have seen that in hard rating questions, there are 2
things that happen. One, that they say you have to do
this and that, but actually, don't have to do anything.
Because, the answer comes greedily. You can do it in 
less or equal to the given moves without any problem.
Or, you have to use the formula they give and convert it
into another formula that you can use to solve the
problem very easily. These are seen again and again in
harder problems.
